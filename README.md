# Vampyr-Puzzle-2D
Try to complete the matches and progress indefinitely.

# Game Description
In a box with an infinite loop, you will only have your number of moves and your number of targets.

Additionally, your bleeding time will be shown in the bottom left and a hungry vampire in the bottom right.

Our goal is to escape the vampire before we run out of moves and the blood fills up.

We have 4 different objects and 3 different specials.

Red,Green,Blue and Yellow <--> Blood Drop,Bat and Vampyr

Explanation;

1-)3-way normal match

2-)If there are 4 or more horizontal and vertical drops, blood will form

3-)If it is square-shaped, a bat is formed

4-)If it is in the shape of a T or L, a vampire is formed

Matches;

Blood Drop X Normal Tyle-->It eliminates whichever color is currently on the grid

Blood Drop X Blood Drop-->It randomly selects 2 colors and eliminates them

Blood Drop X Bat-->At that moment, 3 random normal types on the grid transform into a drop of blood

Blood Drop X Vampyr-->It destroys a 3x3 area

Bat X Normal Tyle-->It transforms normal styes into drops of blood when it merges with them

Bat X Bat-->A total of 10 bats are created, then 10 are randomly eliminated

Bat X Vampyr-->A total of 20 bats are created, then 20 are randomly eliminated

Vampyr X Normal Tyle-->It creates bats in 3 random locations

Vampyr X Vampyr-->It eliminates the entire grid
